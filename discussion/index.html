What is CEU?

CEU is Centro Escolar University one of the biggest, private university in Manila, Philippines.

CEU is one of the oldest and largest educational institution in the Philippines with several campuses in Manila and other parts of the country. It was founded in 1907 and has been expanded to offer wide range of degree programs and professional development course.
